Collect all the Pokemon in the maze but don’t let Team Rocket catch you on the hunt. If you’re caught, you’ll lose a life and unlike a cat you’ve only got three to spare.

Once you're ready to play, press the blue button to go to the game board. You can start the game and move your pokeball around the maze by using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Happy hunting!
